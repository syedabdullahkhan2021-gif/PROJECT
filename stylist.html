TABISH KHOJA

How and when did you start your career?

Initially, I worked in a law firm for 3 years until I decided to follow my dream and passion for fashion.
In 2011 I stared my career as a fashion stylist and now it’s 2021! In these past 10 years, I have realised
that I fit the best in fashion.

What was your “Yes! I made it! Woohoo” Moment

When the pioneer in Fashion industry Frieha Altaf asked me to assist her in fashion weeks and when
in 2012 I was approached by Nabila to assist her for a Loreal Show, that is when I felt Yes! I have arrived!

A celebrity whom you love working with?

Every face and everybody is differently beautiful in its way and since I love taking up challenges there
are no specific names, but yes, definitely love styling celebrities who are professional, enthusiastic,
forthcoming and align with my vision

What’s your favourite go-to men and women fashion brand?

I believe in collecting pieces from here and there, be it a local store or a big brand as far as it is
catching my attention and is going with my sensibility I add it in my cart. So there’s no specific brand.

YASSER ABDUL AZIZ

How and when did you start your career?

My career initially kicked off in 2014, but 2016 was the year when I started working as a freelance Stylist.

What was your “Yes! I made it! Woohoo,” Moment?

My moment when I felt that YES, I have made it! was when I won the Hum Style Award as an
emerging fashion stylist

A celebrity whom you love working with?

Well, I have a lot of celebrity friends that I love working with but the few names I can
quote right away are Maya Ali, Saba Qamar and Hania Amir.

A word that describes your style?

I would say that my style has never been constant, it keeps changing and it has evolved a
lot. Sometimes its athleisure, most of the times it’s street-chic and as I love adding glitter and glam
so yeah BLING!

A celebrity whom you love working with?

What’s your favourite go-to men and women fashion brand?

RAO ALI KHAN

How and when did you start your career?

I started my career in 2015 with styling Hamza Ali Abbasi, Feroze Khan, Sajal Aly And Shahroze Sabzwari
for Hum Style Awards in Dubai. Before that, I was doing a bit of my styling work in Dubai. I did
an editorial shoot with Rohit Bal and was very delighted to see it in the magazine.

What was your “Yes! I made it! Woohoo,” Moment?

As I m not just the stylist I’m also a journalist. when I got the chance to interview Madhuri Dixit and
talk to her that is when I felt this the reason I’m here for! That was my woohoo moment

A celebrity whom you love working with?

I love working with Fawad Khan, also I loved working with Bilal Ashraf in the film “Superstar”.

What’s your favourite go-to men and women fashion brand?

Tbh I don’t have any favourite go-to brand.
It all depends, when Im I out there I pick different things from different designers around the
world but if I have to choose one there’s this brand called “Reserved” I prefer it personally.

AMAL QADRI

How and when did you start your career?

I started in 2014 with personal styling, consulting women with their existing and new wardrobe
according to their lifestyle, did my very first shoot for Sanober Azfar and ended up commenting on

Onboard for a morning show in Dawn channel and then there was no turning back.

What was your “Yes! I made it! Woohoo,” Moment?

Well, there are few, one I can think off right now is styling Aditi Rao Hyderi & Elnaaz Nourouzi.

A celebrity whom you love working with?

Anoushey Ashraf & Syra Yousuf

What’s your favourite go-to men and women fashion brand?

They change every season!
